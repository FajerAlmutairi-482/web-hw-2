This website will be your savior whenever you are not sure what to watch, At the first page i will post most popular films of all genrs and all times. Second page is more organized with my faviort movies listed with others of the same type. I hope you enjoy and find it helpfull.

MAD MAX (2015)

In a post-apocalyptic wasteland, a woman rebels against a tyrannical ruler in search for her homeland with the aid of a group of female prisoners, a psychotic worshiper, and a drifter named Max. Director: George Miller

PULPFICTION (1994)

The lives of two mob hitmen, a boxer, a gangster and his wife, and a pair of diner bandits intertwine in four tales of violence and redemption. Director: Quentin Tarantino

INTERSTELLAR (2014)

A team of explorers travel through a wormhole in space in an attempt to ensure humanity's survival. Director: Christopher Nolan

DJANGO (2012)

With the help of a German bounty hunter, a freed slave sets out to rescue his wife from a brutal Mississippi plantation owner. Director: Quentin Tarantino

JAWS (1975)

When a killer shark unleashes chaos on a beach community, it's up to a local sheriff, a marine biologist, and an old seafarer to hunt the beast down. Director: Steven Spielberg

THE FIFTH ELEMNTS (1997)

In the colorful future, a cab driver unwittingly becomes the central figure in the search for a legendary cosmic weapon to keep Evil and Mr. Zorg at bay. Director: Luc Besson

FIGHT CLUB (1999)

An insomniac office worker and a devil-may-care soapmaker form an underground fight club that evolves into something much, much more. Director: David Fincher

THE BIRDS (1963)

A wealthy San Francisco socialite pursues a potential boyfriend to a small Northern California town that slowly takes a turn for the bizarre when birds of all kinds suddenly begin to attack people. Director: Alfred Hitchcock

2001 A SPACE ODYSSEY (1968)

After discovering a mysterious artifact buried beneath the Lunar surface, mankind sets off on a quest to find its origins with help from intelligent supercomputer H.A.L. 9000. Director: Stanley Kubrick

those specific movies are highly recommended. All been given to us by great directors over the years. in fact you can't find any movie lover who have not seen them. make your popcorn &drink and enjoy a piece of art.
